Shortbread Cookie Recipe!

Learn how to make traditional shortbread cookies!

Ingridents

  • 250 ml of butter at room temperature
  • 125 ml of icing suagr
  • 125 ml of cornstarch
  • 300 ml of flour

Tools

  • Rubber spatula
  • Metal spatula
  • Large mixing bowl
  • 4 teaspoons
  • Dry mixing cups
  • Whisk
  • Electric Mixer
  • Fork
  • 2 wire racks
  • 2 medium baking sheets
  • Parchment paper
  • Oven mits
  • Stove
  • Kitchen timer

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350ºF (177ºC)
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter until creamy with an electric mixer. If you don't have a mixer, beat vigorously by hand.
  3. Add the icing sugar and continue beating until fluffy.
  4. Scrape down the bowl and add the cornstarch and flour, beating just until combined.
  5. Using a spoon, scoop out a ball of the dough and drop onto an ungreased or parchment lined baking sheet 2-5 cm apart. Makes approximatley 12 cookies.
  6. Press gently with a fork and placed candied cherry, chocolate or sprinkles of each unbaked cookie.
  7. Bake for 10 - 12 minutes. Do not over bake.
  8. remove from baking sheets and cool on racks. May be frozen.
  9. these are delicate, so gently put them into a ait-tighy container or on a plate for serving.
